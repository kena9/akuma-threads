1. Who We Are

Olly Threads ("we," "us," "our") is an independent anime artist clothing brand operated by Oliver Jin Wang. We sell original graphic apparel through this website. Our email for privacy-related matters is contact@ollythreads.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or place an order:

  • Name and email address

We do not collect or store payment card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by our payment processor under their own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

4. Print-on-Demand Fulfillment

Orders are fulfilled through our print-on-demand partner (Printify). Your name and shipping address are shared with Printify solely for the purpose of producing and delivering your order. Printify processes this data under their own privacy policy.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly necessary for site functionality (authentication, cart state). We do not use advertising or analytics cookies at this time. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect site functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and order history for as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@ollythreads.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at contact@ollythreads.com.
