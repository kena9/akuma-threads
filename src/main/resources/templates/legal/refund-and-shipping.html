Shipping Policy

Production Time

All Olly Threads items are made-to-order through our print-on-demand partner. Production typically takes 3–7 business days before shipment. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Domestic Shipping (USA)

Standard shipping: 5–10 business days after production. Express options may be available at checkout.

International Shipping

International shipping is available to most countries. Estimated delivery is 10–20 business days after production, depending on destination and customs processing. Customers are responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees.

Tracking

A tracking number will be emailed to you once your order ships. You can also view order status in your account under Orders.

Return & Refund Policy

Made-to-Order Notice

Because all items are printed on-demand specifically for you, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges for buyer's remorse or size errors. Please review our size chart carefully before ordering.

Defective or Damaged Items

If your item arrives defective, damaged in transit, or with a print error, we will replace it at no cost to you. Contact us within 14 days of delivery with your order number and a clear photo of the issue at contact@ollythreads.com.

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, contact us with your order number and a photo. We will reship the correct item and arrange return of the wrong one at no cost to you.

Lost Packages

If your package is confirmed lost by the carrier, we will reship your order or issue a full refund. Packages are considered lost after 30 days (domestic) or 45 days (international) past the ship date with no tracking movement.

How to Request a Refund or Replacement

Email contact@ollythreads.com with subject line "Order #[your order number] — [issue type]". Include your name, order number, and a photo of the item if applicable. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
